## Notice A — Signup Notice

*Displayed to all new users during account registration, immediately above the Create Account button.*

---

### Before You Create Your Account

**First Whistle** collects information to help you manage your sports team or league — things like your name, email address, and team information. Here's what you should know before you sign up:

**What we collect:** Your name, email, role (coach, admin, or player), and any team or player information you add to the platform.

**How we use it:** To show schedules, manage rosters, send game reminders, and enable team communications. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

**Children on the platform:** If you add players under 13 to your team, you are responsible for obtaining parental consent before creating their profiles. Players under 13 cannot create their own accounts.

**Your choices:** You can update or delete your account at any time. To delete a player profile, use the roster management tools or contact us.

**Third-party services:** We use Firebase (Google) to store data and send notifications. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

By clicking **"Create Account,"** you agree to First Whistle's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
