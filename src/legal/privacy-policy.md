# Privacy Policy

**First Whistle**
Effective Date: 2026-03-24
Last Updated: 2026-03-24

---

## Introduction

First Whistle ("we," "us," or "our") is a sports scheduling and team management platform built for youth and adult leagues. We take your privacy — and especially the privacy of children — seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your data.

This policy applies to all users of First Whistle, including coaches, league managers, parents, and players. If you are a parent or guardian of a child using this platform, please read this policy carefully, particularly the **Children's Privacy** section.

If you have questions, contact us at legal@firstwhistle.com.

---

## 1. Who This Policy Covers

This policy applies to:

- **Coaches and team staff** — adults who create accounts and manage teams
- **League managers and administrators** — adults who manage league-level operations
- **Parents and guardians** — adults who consent on behalf of minor players
- **Adult players** — players age 18 and over who hold their own accounts
- **Minor players** — players under 18 whose profiles are created and managed by a coach, admin, or parent

---

## 2. What Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you register for a First Whistle account, we collect:

- Full name
- Email address
- Password (stored in encrypted form via Firebase Authentication)
- Role (coach, league manager, parent, or player)
- Phone number (optional at registration; may be required for SMS notifications)

### 2.2 Player Profile Information

When a coach or administrator creates a player profile — including for minor players — the following information may be entered:

- Player's full name
- Date of birth or age
- Jersey number or position (optional)
- Parent or guardian name(s)
- Parent or guardian email address(es)
- Parent or guardian phone number(s)
- Team and league affiliation

### 2.3 Attendance and Participation Records

We collect records of:

- RSVP responses (yes, no, maybe) submitted by coaches, parents, or players for games and practices
- Attendance check-ins or check-outs logged by coaches or admins
- Participation history tied to a player's profile

### 2.4 Communications Data

When you use First Whistle's messaging and notification features, we may store:

- Messages sent through the platform between coaches, parents, and players
- Email and SMS notification content and delivery status
- Announcement history associated with a team or league

### 2.5 Photos (Future Feature)

First Whistle intends to support team and player photo uploads in a future release. When that feature is available, this policy will be updated. **No photos are collected at this time.** When photo collection is introduced:

- Photos uploaded to team profiles or rosters will be stored on our servers
- Photos of minor players will require parental consent before upload
- Parents may request removal of their child's photo at any time

### 2.6 Usage and Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information when you use the platform:

- Browser type and version
- Device type and operating system
- IP address
- Pages visited and actions taken within the app
- Login timestamps

This data is used to maintain and improve the platform and is not used to build individual advertising profiles.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the service** — create and display schedules, rosters, and team information
- **Send notifications** — deliver game reminders, schedule changes, and announcements via email and SMS
- **Track attendance** — record RSVPs and participation for coaches and league administrators
- **Enable communications** — allow coaches to message parents and players within the platform
- **Support account management** — let users log in, update their profiles, and manage their team memberships
- **Maintain platform security** — detect and prevent unauthorized access or abuse
- **Improve the product** — analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and build better features
- **Respond to support requests** — address questions or issues you contact us about

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to serve you third-party advertising.

## 4. Children's Privacy — COPPA Compliance

This section applies specifically to players under the age of 13 and their parents or guardians.

### 4.1 Our Approach to Children's Data

First Whistle complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which governs the collection of personal information from children under 13 in the United States. We do not allow children under 13 to create their own accounts. Player profiles for children under 13 are created and managed by a verified adult — a coach, league administrator, or parent.

### 4.2 What Data We Hold on Children Under 13

For a child under 13, a player profile may contain:

- The child's name
- The child's date of birth or age
- Jersey number or position (optional)
- Team and league affiliation
- Attendance and RSVP records tied to team events

**We do not collect** a child's email address, phone number, or login credentials. Communication is directed to the parent or guardian contact on file.

### 4.3 Parental Consent

By adding a player under 13 to a team, the coach or administrator represents that they have obtained verifiable parental or guardian consent to create that player's profile and to share the associated parent contact information with First Whistle. Parents who believe their child's profile was created without their consent should contact us immediately at legal@firstwhistle.com.

Parents may contact us to:

- Review the information we hold about their child
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of their child's profile and all associated data

We will respond to verified parental requests within **30 days**.

### 4.4 Players Age 13–17

Players age 13 through 17 may be added to team rosters and given access to a limited player account. For platform accounts held by users age 13–17, we require parental consent before account activation. Parents may contact us at any time to review, correct, or delete their minor child's account.

### 4.5 How to Submit a Parental Request

To exercise any parental right over your child's data, contact us at legal@firstwhistle.com with the subject line "Parental Data Request." Please include your name, your child's name and team, and the action you are requesting. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing the request.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Within your team or league** — coaches and league admins can see player roster data for their own team or league only
- **With service providers** — as described in Section 5, solely for delivering the service
- **With your consent** — if you explicitly authorize a specific disclosure
- **For legal compliance** — if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users or the public
- **In a business transfer** — if First Whistle is acquired or merges with another entity, user data may be transferred with prior notice to affected users

---

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

### 7.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services to your organization.

### 7.2 Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time through the platform settings or by contacting us at legal@firstwhistle.com. When an account is deleted, your personal account information is removed from active systems within **30 days**. Backup copies may persist for up to **90 days** before being purged.

### 7.3 Player Profile Deletion

Coaches, league admins, and parents (for minor players) may request deletion of a player profile. The player's profile and associated attendance records are removed from active systems within **30 days** of a verified request.

### 7.4 Organization / League Deletion

When an organization is closed, all associated team and player data is permanently removed within **90 days**.

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), encrypted password storage via Firebase Authentication, and role-based access controls. No system is completely secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at legal@firstwhistle.com.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

| Right | Description |
|---|---|
| **Access** | Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you |
| **Correction** | Request correction of inaccurate data |
| **Deletion** | Request that we delete your data |
| **Portability** | Request your data in a machine-readable format |
| **Objection** | Object to certain types of processing |
| **Withdrawal of Consent** | Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time |

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at legal@firstwhistle.com. We will respond within **30 days**.

**California residents (CCPA):** You have the right to know what data we collect, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data).

**EEA users (GDPR):** We process data on the lawful basis of contract performance and, where required, consent. You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.
